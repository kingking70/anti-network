about

i wanted to create a more authentic way for people to meet through my own network. each person is vetted by me.

i've decided to use this project to list my friends and my unrealised dreams and ideas; to somewhat promote some sort of accountability and see if we could help one another overcome the challenges or hurdles we foresee.

at initialisation, the vision is to fulfil our most selfish ideas ethically. i believe altruism can only be attained by putting ourselves first. being selfish is the first step to being selfless.

upon expansion, the goal is to be a platform for members to participate through investment — physical, mental or financial. there will be tools available to fulfil members' ideas, be it hardware or software. the details will be refined subsequently as we grow, slowly. ideally, a good portion of any profit will go to growing the next ethical selfless idea.

there's no big philosophical or financial ambition here. i just want the members — my friends — to enjoy a full life. one that they could call theirs.
